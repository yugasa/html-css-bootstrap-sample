What is Loan Sahyog and how is it better?

Loan Sahyog is a technology platform which assists individuals to borrow and lend money from other individuals.

For the borrower the platform will assist to provide access to loans that are affordable, faster, fairer and transparent on rates and charges

If you are a lender, we will give you access to quality borrowers to whom you can grant loans on your own terms.
